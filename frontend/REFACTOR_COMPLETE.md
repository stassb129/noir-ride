- Letter spacing: -0.02em for headings

- Max width: 1200px
- Clear hierarchy

### 4. **Removed All**
- ❌ Emojis
- ❌ Colorful icons
- ❌ Generic UI cards
- ❌ Clutter and noise
- ❌ Unnecessary text (40% reduction)
