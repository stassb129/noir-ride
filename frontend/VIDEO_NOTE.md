# Background Video Note

## Hero Section Video

The Hero section is designed to use a background video for maximum visual impact. 

### Video Placement
Add your luxury car video file to the `public` folder:
- **Path**: `/public/luxury-car-video.mp4`
- **Recommended specs**:
  - Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
  - Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
  - Duration: 10-30 seconds (looping)
  - File size: < 5MB (optimized for web)

### Placeholder
